# HelpAProduct — Assistant Knowledge Base

Everything the phone assistant is allowed to state about the business lives here.
The assistant answers ONLY from what's below; for anything not covered it takes a
message instead of guessing. Restart the app after editing (or it reloads on the
next call).

## About
HelpAProduct builds custom software, AI agents, and ecommerce systems — designed to
solve real problems and built to last. We're a small US-based product team led by
Ben (Industrial & Operations Engineering, University of Michigan) and Eric (Computer
Science, USC), with a tight group of engineers.

## What we do
- **Custom software** — AI assistants, dashboards, automations, and integrations
  built around how you actually work.
- **Ecommerce growth** — listings, advertising, and operations management that move
  the needle.
- **Ongoing support** — we maintain, improve, and scale what we build with you.

Our stance: if you can describe the problem, we can build the solution. Tell us what
you need and we'll figure out how to ship it.

## What we've built (representative projects)
- **ZenCub** — native iOS app that turns BJJ technique videos into step-by-step breakdowns.
- **Amazon Ads Command Center** — multi-tenant dashboard pulling live SP-API and Ads API data.
- **ThrivePact** — habit accountability app with daily check-ins via web, SMS, or iOS.
- **PopPublic** — Chrome extension that autofills marketplace listings (Facebook, Craigslist, Nextdoor).
- **Market Intelligence Dashboard** — flags stocks diverging from their peer groups; scans Reddit/Stocktwits.
- **Social Engagement Ops Dashboard** — community management across platforms with a human approval workflow.
- **Nonprofit Member Directory** — searchable directory with private/public data separation.
- **Interactive demos & automations** — clickable demos of voice AI, CRM, and dashboards.
- **S-Corp Finance Command Center** — finance dashboard that replaces spreadsheets.
- **Bazaarvoice Review Response Console** — review scraping with AI-drafted responses.
- **No Slop** — AI writing-quality detection and rewriting engine.
- **Consumer brands portfolio** — our own products grown on Amazon.
